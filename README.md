##  How It Works

1. **Enter your details** - Age, height, current weight, target weight, and timeline
2. **Get your targets** - The app calculates your BMR, TDEE, and daily calorie target
3. **Track progress** - Your calculations are saved to view later

### Calculation Method

- **BMR** - Calculated using the Harris-Benedict equation
- **TDEE** - Estimated using BMR √ó 1.55 (moderate activity)
- **Calorie Adjustment** - Based on 7,700 calories per kg of body weight
